Let's go camping.. :)

Camping and campers form much less of the landscape in India than they do in places like Europe, the US and Australia. But there is, nevertheless, a dedicated, enthusiastic and growing bunch of campers who are looking to make the most of India’s natural beauty by setting up camp in out-of-the-way places, as far away from civilization as possible. Indeed, India has plenty of beauty spots for the happy camper – and the adventurous one.



Dusk till dawn..!

If the idea of sleeping in a tent doesn’t appeal to you, but traditional campers are out-of-reach financially, perhaps it’s time to try van camping. Van camping is just what it sounds like—camping in a van. While some camper vans are decked out with small kitchens, some use stripped out vans with an air mattress in the back. Some camper vans can sleep up to five people!



Make it worth...!

Camping is a great outdoor bonding activity with family and friends. While camping with kids involves plenty of work, it’s great to get out there and spend some quality time with them in the woods. This type of activity leaves you connected to nature and your loved ones. Camping is a lot of fun, especially if you go with good friends who enjoy it like you do. Sitting around the campfire, sharing stories and being in the moment with no distractions. It's a great opportunity to build relationships and really get to know each other
